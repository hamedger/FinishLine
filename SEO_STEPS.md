# Finish Line SEO Steps

This checklist is focused on ranking better for:
- `oil change livonia`
- `oil change near me`
- `auto repair livonia`

Domain: `https://finishlineoil.com`

---

## 1) Launch & Verify Website SEO (Do First)

- [ ] Deploy latest website changes to production.
- [ ] Open `https://finishlineoil.com/robots.txt` and confirm it loads.
- [ ] Open `https://finishlineoil.com/sitemap.xml` and confirm it loads.
- [ ] Open `https://finishlineoil.com/oil-change-livonia-mi` and confirm page is live.
- [ ] Ensure phone button and direction links work on mobile.

Success check:
- Sitemap and robots are accessible publicly.
- Main page + oil change page are both live.

---

## 2) Google Search Console Setup

- [ ] Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
- [ ] Add property for `finishlineoil.com` (Domain property preferred).
- [ ] Verify ownership (DNS TXT record).
- [ ] Submit sitemap: `https://finishlineoil.com/sitemap.xml`.
- [ ] Request indexing for:
  - [ ] `https://finishlineoil.com/`
  - [ ] `https://finishlineoil.com/oil-change-livonia-mi`
- [ ] Check Coverage/Pages report after 48-72 hours.

Success check:
- Property verified.
- Submitted sitemap shows discovered URLs.

---

## 3) Google Business Profile (Highest Impact for Local SEO)

- [ ] Open/claim listing in [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com/).
- [ ] Primary category: `Oil change service`.
- [ ] Secondary category: `Auto repair shop`.
- [ ] Confirm NAP matches website exactly:
  - Name: Finish Line Oil Change and Repair
  - Address: 11900 Middlebelt Rd, Livonia, MI 48150
  - Phone: (734) 367-6457
- [ ] Confirm business hours exactly match website.
- [ ] Add 15-20 real photos (front sign, bays, team, waiting area).
- [ ] Add service list (Conventional, Synthetic Blend, Full Synthetic, High-Mileage).
- [ ] Publish 1 GBP post weekly (coupon, reminder, seasonal tip).
- [ ] Add and answer Q&A on profile.
- [ ] Copy your GBP review link and replace `googleReviewUrl` in `src/lib/constants.ts`.

Success check:
- Listing is complete and verified.
- Categories and NAP are fully aligned with website.

---

## 4) Review Growth System (Critical for Top 3 Map Pack)

- [ ] Ask every happy customer for a Google review at checkout.
- [ ] Send SMS with review link same day service is done.
- [ ] Add QR code at counter pointing to GBP review link.
- [ ] Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 48 hours.
- [ ] Target minimum: 5+ new reviews per month.

Response template (simple):
- Thank customer by name.
- Mention service (oil change, brakes, etc.).
- Mention Livonia/location naturally.
- Invite them back.

Success check:
- Review count and review velocity increase monthly.

---

## 5) Citation Consistency (Off-Page Authority)

- [ ] Ensure identical NAP on:
  - [ ] Yelp
  - [ ] Apple Maps
  - [ ] Bing Places
  - [ ] Facebook
  - [ ] Local directories/chamber listings
- [ ] Remove duplicates/wrong phone/address listings.
- [ ] Keep URL as `https://finishlineoil.com` everywhere.

Success check:
- No major citation mismatch.

## Notes

- Local SEO is mostly won through **Google Business Profile + reviews + NAP consistency**.
- Website optimization helps, but GBP activity and review growth drive Map Pack rankings.
- If old domain `finishlineoilchange.com` exists, keep a 301 redirect to `finishlineoil.com`.
